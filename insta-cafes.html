    Botanica+Co Cafe

    Botanica+Co Café is the most gorgeous café in Bangsar South (KL) Every corner is a picturesque spot, every inch of the café is filled with greenery. Inspired by the botanicals, the impression of eating outside in the lush greenery is caught inside. Being one of the most Instagrammable cafés in Kuala Lumpur, the warmth of the front doorway, particularly the orchids, relaxes and enlivens the visitor. Get a cuppa or a cocktail drink and enjoy in this lovely environment. This is a pork-free place yet includes alcohol.

    Location:
    G5, 8, Jalan Kerinchi, Bangsar South, 59200 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur
    Website:
    www.botanica.com.my
    Operating Hours:
    11.30 am - 10.00 pm (Monday - Wednesday)
    11.30 am - 12.00 am (Friday)
    9.30 am - 12.00 am (Saturday)
    9.30 am - 10.00 pm (Sunday)

    +6016-965 6422

    Wildflowers KL

    Wildflowers KL is a new restaurant and bar situated on the busy Petaling Street. The restaurant is situated in a refurbished heritage store, redecorated with a new, sleek bar on the ground floor. Asian fusion restaurant is located in the corner of Petaling Street. The upper dining room has a glass ceiling that allows natural light to spill in. The menu includes snacks, small plates, greens, carbs, proteins and desserts as well as craft cocktails. Be prepared for a foodgasm but DO NOT forget to click photos. It could be one of those photos on Instagram to garner more attention from your followers.

    Location:
    153, Jalan Petaling, City Centre, 50000 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours:
    12.00 pm - 12.00 am (Friday - Sunday)
    12.00 pm - 10.30 pm (Monday - Thursday)
    Closed on Tuesday
    Phone:
    +603-2856 3369

    A Pie Thing

    This quaint café in Damansara Uptown can be instantly noticed thanks to its classic black-and-white signboard. A Pie Thing will have you and your friend covered for sweet and savoury varieties. The café serves a wide range of sweet and savoury pies filled with everything you can think of-from chilli cheese to steak, apple crumbles, marshmallow and vanilla ice cream. The pies are definitely Instagram-worthy.

    Location:
    128 G, Jalan SS 21/35, Damansara Utama, 47400 Petaling Jaya, Selangor
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 9.00 pm (Tuesday - Sunday)
    Closed on Monday
    Phone:
    +6012-951 5233

    Therefore PJ

    A contemporary casual cafe aims to bring people together for tasty snacks and tasty brews in an elegant, sophisticated space. The design of a café\'s interior is as important as the food and drinks it provides. This café in the Klang Valley oozes a sleek and dreamlike feeling that we completely love. With grapefruit walls and polka-dotted chairs, this is the best place for a girl\'s day out. The menu contains both Western and fusion dishes such as rendang taco and nangka nachos.

    Urban Daybreak

    Urban Daybreak is a cosy brunch spot that started off in Penang. The menu features a selection of Australian-inspired breakfast and brunch dishes from avocado toast to tasty croissants and sandwiches. Not only does their food taste good, but their plating is so beautiful that you will want to take pictures for the gram. They also make their own sausages, ice cream and peanut butter!

    Location:
    11, Jalan Telawi 5, Bangsar, 59100 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours:
    8.00 am - 7.30 pm (Daily)
    Phone:

    Bukku Cafe

    Following a minimalistic theme, Bukku Cafe is a warm and cosy little alcove that offers a sweet respite away from the bustling city. As the name implies (‘buku’ translates to ‘book’), one of the attractions here is the arrays of shelves filled with colorful books. Additionally, the modest backyard is the go-to seating spot here as this alfresco dining area is also shaded by a canopy of trees.

    Location:
    1, Jalan Singgahsana 4A/KU2, Bandar Bukit Raja, 41050 Klang, Selangor
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 5.00 pm (Monday - Sunday)
    Phone:
    +6012-567 7392

    Florescence by World of Phalaenopsis Cafe

    Hidden away in the Ulu Yam branch of World of Phalaenopsis lies the magnificent Florescence by World of Phalaenopsis Cafe. WIth a serene duck-filled pond outside, this ultra aesthetically-pleasing to the peepers establishment is filled to the brim with flourishing orchids along with other greenery. In terms of the menu, expect local classics such as mee hoon siam, nasi lemak rendang ayam and kaya toast.

    Nippori Bistro

    As one of the very recent cafes that have popped out on the block, Nippori Bistro is a trendy establishment that perfectly blends the best of two culinary worlds: French and Japanese. With a fusion menu in tow, the decor includes Japanese-style floor seatings and European-style high ceilings. However, the main attraction here seen on every Insta feed is the huge caricature-like portrait of a plump Mona Lisa.

    Cafe Tujoh

    Situated within Section 17, feel free to spend a relaxing afternoon in Café Tujoh’s verdant courtyard that’s brightened by the sunlight seeping through glass windows. In terms of the decor, there are antique furniture and electronics along with monochrome artworks hung on the walls. The menu consists of a fusion between Western and Asian cuisines.
