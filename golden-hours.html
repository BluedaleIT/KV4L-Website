    Places of Worship

    Bukit Tabur

    Bukit Tabur is home to one of the most beautiful sunrises in Malaysia. Situated between Taman Melawati and UIA Gombak, Bukit Tabur is a lovely location for hikers due to its breathtaking view of the Klang Gate Dam and the Kuala Lumpur landscape.

    Read More

    There are many trails that lead to different peaks at Bukit Tabur. The most popular and longest path is located behind Zoo Negara, at the opening of the Jalan Taman Zooview. The hike is around 3 hours, so plan ahead and you\'ll be greeted with a beautiful sunrise.

    Read Less


    Location:
    53100, Selangor

    Broga Hill

    Broga is located on the border between the states of Negri Sembilan and Selangor. The name of Broga is thought to have been originated from Buragas, a mythical creature.

    Read More

    Well-known for its breathtaking view, Broga Hill is another common landmark for those close and far who come to this place for a stunning view. Unlike Bukit Tabur, there is only one way up and down Broga Hill, with three points of view – at 880 feet, 1150 feet, and at the top at 1312 feet. The hike can take between 40 and 60 minutes. How fast you reach the summit totally depends on your fitness level. After coming down from Broga, you can enjoy a refreshing drink at the nearby coconut stall. If you choose to hike in the morning, be prepared to be greeted by a beautiful sunrise.

    Raed Less


    Location:
    Jalan Broga, 43500 Semenyih, Selangor

    Tadom Hill, Selangor

    Tadom Hills is the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. The best way to spend an afternoon is to plunge into Tadom Emerald Lake or sit in one of the bamboo gazebos.

    Read More

    After a full day of Tarzan-swinging and platform-jumping, take a break and watch the golden sun vanish over the horizon. Nature lovers aren’t the only ones who can take advantage of Mother Nature’s painting but it will also be the perfect back for your pre-wedding shoots. The sunset is sure to add on to the romantic feels to your photos.

    Taman Tasik Titiwangsa

    Taman Tasik Titiwangsa lies in the centre of the city. It is a leisure park with a wide lake as its main drawing factor. Famous among city dwellers, it offers tennis courts, a playground for children and other leisure facilities for water sports, jogging and the like.

    Read More

    Conveniently located nearby the park is the Remote Control (RC) car track, which is complete with a driver platform and seats for spectators, making it a common activity for RC fans on weekends. The Park also provides amenities such as bicycle riding, helicopter rides, tennis courts, jogging and water sports. It's a common gathering place for city dwellers on the weekend, particularly for sunrise and sunset in Kuala Lumpur.

    Taman Tasik Ampang Hilir

    Taman Tasik Ampang Hilir acts as a green lung for the local area. The park has a well-maintained cycle and jogging path along with exercise facilities for gym freaks. There is also a children's playground and a 2.7 km walkway.

    Read More

    Taman Tasik Ampang Hilir also serves as one of the best spots to watch sunsets in Kuala Lumpur. You will enjoy a gentle stroll around the lake. It's always better to enjoy the golden hour by watching the sunset in between, as the city transitions from day-to-night. It's amazing to watch the sunrise and the sunset over Kuala Lumpur\'s tall buildings!

    Pantai Morib

    Pantai Morib is one of the closest beaches to Kuala Lumpur. It can get busy on weekends with day-trippers. When the tide is low you can see small tiny crabs running on the muddy beach. It is a good place to take a stroll by the sea on the roads.

    Read More

    Efforts have been made in recent years to provide more beach facilities. There is a food court, clean toilets and showers, a children\'s playground and a kite flying field. Kites can be bought and roller blades are also available for hire.
