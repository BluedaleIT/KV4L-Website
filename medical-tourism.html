    Hospital Serdang

    Opened in 2006, the Serdang Hospital is a government-funded multi-speciality hospital located in the, Sepang district of Selangor, Malaysia. This hospital is situated near Putrajaya, the Malaysian federal government administrative centre. Serdang Hospital is a reference point for clinics and hospitals in the vicinity, particularly those from Serdang, Kajang, Putrajaya, Dengkil and Puchong. Serdang Hospital uses the IT hospital setting called the Total Hospital Information System (THIS).

    Location:
    Jalan Puchong, 43000 Kajang, Selangor

    Colombia Asia Hospital

    Columbia Asia Hospital is a multi-specialty hospital situated off Jalan Genting Klang on Jalan Danau Saujana. Modern medical facilities provided are specialised care such as Cardiology, Ear, Nose, & Throat, among others.

    Location:
    No 1, Jalan Danau Saujana, Off Jalan Genting Klang, 53300 Kuala, Malaysia

    Pantai Hospital Klang

    Pantai Hospital Klang has been well known for the past 30 years as a quality and affordable provider of health services to the Klang community. The centre was founded for the first time in January 1981 by 9 specialist consultants and began with 60 beds. It is now operating in a purpose-built hospital with 108 beds. The hospital is accredited by the Malaysian Society for Health Quality (MSQH).

    Location:
    Lot 5921, Persiaran Raja Muda Musa, Taman Radzi, 41200 Klang, Selangor

    Thompson Hospital Kota Damansara

    Founded in 2008, Thomson Hospital Kota Damansara (THKD) is the main hospital of TMC Life Sciences Berhad (TMCLS) with a wide range of specialities. Located in the prime alcove of Kota Damansara, THKD is well connected by road to Kuala Lumpur and also to Kota Damansara MRT station. At present, Thomson Hospital Kota Damansara has a capacity of 205 beds, more than 100 trained medical and surgical consultants, a brand new block furnished with 600 new beds, 10 state-of-the-art operating theatres, 154 outpatient clinics and more innovative facilities are set to come in early 2021.

    Location:
    11, Jalan Teknologi, Pju 5 Kota Damansara, 47810 Petaling Jaya, Selangor

    Institut Jantung Negara

    Founded in 1992, Institut Jantung Negara is a reliable heart specialist institution that offers the latest technological advancements and treatments in cardiovascular and thoracic medicine. The cardiology services include invasive and non-invasive procedures such as coronary angiograms, angioplasty, stenting, cardiac resynchronisation therapy, radiofrequency ablation, atrial fibrillation, echocardiography and much more.
