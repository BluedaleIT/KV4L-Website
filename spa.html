    Spas in Klang Valley

    Khareyana Spa

    Offering a variety of unique spa treatments from head to toe, guests can enjoy a one-of-a-kind pampering session with highly qualified therapists who use only the best natural traditional ingredients renowned for their rejuvenating and healing effects on the mind, body and spirit. From the lemongrass scent to the sounds of running water and light music in the background, guests are teleported in an instant to another realm, making this spa a success.

    Location:
    36, Jalan Selangor, Pjs 7, 40650 Petaling Jaya, Selangor
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 8.00 pm (Monday - Sunday)
    Phone:
    +603-7958 6219

    Lavish Spa

    Nestled in the heart of Kuala Lumpur within Fahrenheit 88, Lavish Spa offers a holistic sanctuary for those eager to escape the tedium of daily life. Customers can take a dip in the outdoor swimming pool, relax in the jacuzzi, or work out an invigorating workout in a fully equipped gym, before indulging in the talents of experienced therapists for a relaxing massage or a mani-pedi service. Other facilities offered include a movie theatre, a lounge room, and a cafe serving international cuisine to soak up appetites between sampling sessions.

    Location:
    Lot 5-02-06, 179, Jalan Gading, Bukit Bintang, 55100 Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours:
    Open for 24 hours
    Phone:
    +603-2148 5888

    Swasana Spa

    Swasana Spa offers a range of seven body massages in the form of traditional Balinese, Thai and Malayan treatments. All massages are built to offer guests the ultimate rejuvenation and indulgence. Swasana Spa has been accredited as a 5-star resort by the Ministry of Tourism of Malaysia, the recipient of the World Luxury Spa Award and several other awards.

    Location:
    Impiana @ Klcc Hotel, No. 13, Jalan Pinang, Kuala Lumpur, 50450 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 10.00 pm (Monday - Sunday)
    Phone:
    +603-2147 1111

    Thai Odyssey

    Thai Odyssey was founded in 2004 and has since spread to more than 140 outlets across the world. The spa provides a variety of Thai massages including conventional massage, aromatherapy, herbal therapy and foot massage for both men and women. In a serene environment full of traditional Thai décor, the atmosphere is designed to help you relax as experienced masseurs trained in the art of Thai massage soothe your tight muscles.

    Location:
    1 Utama Shopping Centre (New Wing), B13A, B22 & B26, Basement 1 & Basement 2, 1, Lebuh Bandar Utama, Bandar Utama, 47800 Petaling Jaya
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 10.00 pm (Sunday - Thursday)
    10.00 am - 10.30 pm (Friday & Saturday)
    Phone:
    +603-7710 1663
