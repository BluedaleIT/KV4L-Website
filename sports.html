    I-City Theme Park

    If you are looking for a one-stop amusement centre that offers diverse experiences, head over to I-City Theme Park. It is divided into three main areas which comprise the -8 degrees SnoWalk that was constructed with the help of renowned ice sculptors, City of Digital Lights that is decorated with 1 million multicoloured LED lights and Water World with the first tornado ride in South East Asia.

    Location:
    D-1-G, Jalan Multimedia 7/Ah, I-City, 40000 Shah Alam, Selangor
    Website:
    www.i-city.my
    Operating Hours:
    11.00 am - 12.00 pm (Monday - Thursday)
    11.00 am - 1.00 am (Friday - Sunday)
    Phone:
    +603-5521 8877

    Wet World Water Park Shah Alam

    Get ready to have some fun under the sun at Wet World Water Park Shah Alam. Following a tropical island theme, the exciting slides and rides at the water park include the Super Hurricane, Monsoon Buster, Thunder River, Caribbean Rider and Whirlwind Escape. There are also a few children’s wet playgrounds for the younger children to enjoy.

    Location:
    3A, Persiaran Dato Menteri, Seksyen 14, 40000 Shah Alam, Selangor
    Website:
    wetworld.my
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 6.00 pm (Friday - Monday)
    Closed from Tuesday to Thursday unless it is a school and public holiday
    Phone:
    +603-5513 2020

    Sunway Lagoon

    Offering over 90 different yet equally entertaining attractions spread across 88 acres of land is Sunway Lagoon. It is separated into six adventure zones which consists of the Water Park, Amusement Park, Wildlife Park, Extreme Park, Scream Park and Nickelodeon Lost Lagoon (Asia’s first Nickelodeon themed land filled with classic cartoon characters). Also, one of the world’s longest pedestrian suspension bridges is here.

    Location:
    3, Jalan PJS 11/11, Bandar Sunway, 47500 Subang Jaya, Selangor
    Website:
    sunwaylagoon.com
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 6.00 pm (Monday - Sunday)
    Closed on Tuesday
    Phone:
    +603-5639 0000

    Bangi Wonderland

    Another thrilling water park to splash around in is Bangi Wonderland, stretched across a 12-acre land in Kajang. Offering 14 exciting slides and rides to enjoy, some of them include the Anaconda Trail, Body Slide, Spiralax, Boomeriang, The Fall, Green Pearl, Magic Funnel and the rainbow-coloured Insane Racer. Apart from the Kids Adventure water playground, mellow out at the relaxing Pirates Spa pool and Meander River.

    Location:
    2, Persiaran Bangi Avenue, Bangi Avenue, 43000 Kajang, Selangor
    Website:
    www.bangiwonderland.com.my
    Operating Hours:
    11.00 am - 6.00 pm (Monday, Wednesday - Friday)
    10.00 am - 6.00 pm (Saturday - Sunday)
    Closed on Tuesday
    Phone:
    +6019-682 7174

    AirRider Indoor Skydiving

    Experience the thrill of skydiving without worrying about the frightening fear of jumping off a plane at 14, 000 feet above the ground at AirRider Indoor Skydiving. Housed within 1 Utama Shopping Centre, this simulated experience is equivalent to the real deal but it occurs within a wind tunnel boosting up to 220 km/h for 50 seconds!

    Location:
    1 Utama Shopping Centre, City Centre 1, Lebuh Bandar Utama, Bandar Utama, 47800 Petaling Jaya, Selangor
    Website:
    www.windlab.my
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 10.00 pm (Sunday - Thursday)
    10.00 am - 10.30 pm (Friday - Saturday)
    Phone:
    +603-7728 5588

    TREX Bike Park

    Boasting an elaborate bicycle circuit within a lush landscaped park is the recently developed TREX Bike Park. Apart from clear signs, the layout contains asphalt roads with direction (including merging lanes), back-to-back bumpy BMX tracks and varnished wooden MTB trails. There is also a spacious bike parking area that’s modelled into quirky shapes. Additionally, there is a bicycle shop inside to acquire rentals; mainly mountain bikes and city bikes for a very affordable rate. However, guests are definitely allowed to bring their own. Essentially, the park is designed towards families, cycling novices as well as MTB and BMX practitioners.

    Location:
    Lot 6232, Persiaran Mokhtar Dahari, Eco Grandeur, 42300 Bandar Puncak Alam, Selangor
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 7.00 pm (Tuesday-Friday)
    9.00 am - 7.00 pm (Saturday)
    9.00 am - 8.00 pm (Sunday)
    Phone:
    +6012-393 3890

    Shah Alam Extreme Park

    At Shah Alam Extreme Park, there are a multitude of facilities available such as an inline skate park, bike park (with BMX race course and North Shore elements), wall climbing, paintball arena as well as futsal courts. It is encouraged to bring your own bikes and skateboards. With ample parking space and several fast food joints right next to the facilities, this is the perfect spot to spend the day.

    Location:
    Jalan Lompat Pagar 13/37, Seksyen 13, 40100 Shah Alam, Selangor
    Operating Hours:
    9.00 am - 7.00 pm (Monday - Sunday)
    Phone:
    +6012-287 8787

    Camp5 Climbing Gym

    At Camp 5 Climbing Gym, challenge yourself to take on the five different sections within one of the most popular and trusted boulder-climbing companies in the Klang Valley. Across various outlets, these challenging walls include the 20m Prowall of Camp5 1Utama, the ABC Wall at Camp5 Paradigm, bouldering at Camp5 Utropolis, the Hydraulic Wall and Fitness Studio at Camp5 Eco City.The museum shows jewellery collections, tools, beautiful carvings, and pieces with complex workmanship that have spiritual meaning. Surprisingly, it's more of a shop than a museum where tourists can purchase jades which are imported from Myanmar and China.

    Location:
    5th Floor, 1 Utama Shopping Centre, Lebuh Bandar Utama, Bandar Utama, 47800 Petaling Jaya, Selangor
    Website:
    camp5.com
    Operating Hours:
    12.00 pm - 10.00 pm (Monday - Friday)
    10.00 am - 8.00 pm (Saturday - Sunday)
    Phone:
    +603-7726 0410

    BUMP Bouldering

    With the bold ambition of providing the first premium indoor bouldering, fitness and lifestyle experience of climbing in the country comes BUMP Bouldering. For reference, this is different to rock climbing in the sense that no ropes or harnesses are used to climb these small rock formations or colourful artificial rock walls. There are also spacious viewing areas to rest in while watching your fellow boulderers scale to the top.

    RF Para-venture

    If you have ever thought of gliding in the cooling air over the beautiful natural landscape of grassy hills and lush forests, then paragliding with the help of RF Para-venture is the next activity to scratch off your adventure-filled bucket list. For the first timers, it is best to go for tandem paragliding which involves the assistance of a trained instructor as you jump off the observatory deck at Jugra Hill.

    Location:
    Jalan Bukit Jugra, Jugra Lama, 42700 Banting, Selangor
    Operating Hours:
    Call to book a slot in advance (subject to weather conditions)
    Phone:
    +6011-1002 2081
